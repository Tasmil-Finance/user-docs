<TLDR>
The DeFindex agent manages vault operations on DeFindex protocol. Deposit assets into managed strategy vaults that automatically optimize yield, withdraw your position, and check vault performance.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Deposit** | Add assets to a DeFindex vault | "Deposit 100 USDC into the DeFindex USDC vault" |
| **Withdraw** | Remove assets from a vault | "Withdraw my DeFindex position" |
| **Check Position** | View vault shares and performance | "What's my DeFindex position worth?" |
| **List Vaults** | See available DeFindex vaults | "Show available DeFindex vaults" |

## Deposit into a Vault

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your deposit request.** Example: "Deposit 100 USDC into the DeFindex USDC vault"

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/defindex/step-1-deposit-prompt.png"
      alt="DeFindex deposit request"
      step={1}
      caption="Specify the amount and target vault"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review vault details.** The agent shows: vault strategy, current APY, TVL, and your expected shares.

    <StepScreenshot

      alt="Vault details and APY"
      step={2}
      caption="Review the vault strategy and expected returns"

  </Step>
  <Step>

</Steps>
