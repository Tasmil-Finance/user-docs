<TLDR>
The Phoenix agent handles DEX operations on Phoenix protocol. Execute token swaps with competitive rates, provide liquidity to earn fees, and manage your LP positions.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Swap** | Exchange tokens via Phoenix DEX | "Swap 50 USDC to XLM on Phoenix" |
| **Add Liquidity** | Provide tokens to a Phoenix pool | "Add 100 USDC to Phoenix USDC/XLM pool" |
| **Remove Liquidity** | Withdraw your LP position | "Remove my Phoenix LP" |
| **Check Position** | View your Phoenix LP status | "What's my Phoenix position?" |

## Remove Liquidity

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Request withdrawal.** Example: "Remove my Phoenix USDC/XLM LP"
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review and approve.** See the tokens you'll receive, then sign.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Tokens returned.** Both assets arrive back in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>
