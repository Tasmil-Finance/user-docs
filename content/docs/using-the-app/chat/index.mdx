<TLDR>
The Chat tab is your interface to Tasmil's 13 specialized AI agents. Ask in plain English — the supervisor routes your request to the right agent. Every transaction shows a confirmation card you must approve before anything executes on-chain.
</TLDR>

## How Chat Works

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **You ask.** Type anything in plain English in the chat input at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Supervisor routes.** The routing agent picks the right specialist — a protocol agent for transactions, a research agent for read-only queries, a bridge agent for cross-chain moves.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Agent responds.** Research questions answer inline with data. Transaction requests show an approval card.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **You approve.** Every on-chain action shows a card with simulation results. You inspect it and sign — or reject it. Nothing executes without your signature.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Human-in-the-Loop Card

When an agent proposes an on-chain action, you see a confirmation card showing:

- **What will happen** — Plain-English summary of the transaction
- **Simulation result** — Pre-flight of the Soroban transaction, including fee estimate and expected outcome
- **Approve or Reject** — Sign to proceed, or decline. Nothing happens on-chain if you reject

<Callout type="warn">
**Nothing executes without your explicit signature.** Tasmil agents cannot move funds on their own — they propose, you decide.
</Callout>

## Prompting Tips

- **Be specific about amounts.** "Supply 100 USDC to Blend" works better than "put some money in lending"
- **Mention the asset.** If you hold both USDC and XLM, say which one you mean
- **Ask questions freely.** Research and Info agents are read-only — no signature required
- **Multi-step is fine.** "Check my health factor on Blend, then tell me if I should adjust" routes correctly

See the full prompt recipe collection at [Prompt Recipes](/docs/using-the-app/prompt-recipes).
