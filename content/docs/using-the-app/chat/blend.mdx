<TLDR>
The Blend agent handles all lending/borrowing operations on Blend protocol. You can supply assets to earn yield, borrow against collateral, repay loans, withdraw positions, and claim BLND emission rewards — all through natural language in chat.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Supply** | Deposit assets into a lending pool to earn interest | "Supply 100 USDC to Blend" |
| **Withdraw** | Remove your supplied assets from a pool | "Withdraw 50 USDC from Blend" |
| **Borrow** | Borrow assets against your collateral | "Borrow 200 XLM against my USDC collateral" |
| **Repay** | Pay back borrowed assets | "Repay 100 XLM on Blend" |
| **Claim Rewards** | Harvest BLND emission rewards | "Claim my BLND rewards" |
| **Check Position** | View your supply, borrow, health factor | "What's my Blend position?" |

## Supply Assets (Lending)

Earn interest by supplying USDC or XLM to Blend lending pools.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your supply request.** Example: "Supply 100 USDC to the Blend Fixed pool"

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-1-supply-prompt.png"
      alt="Typing supply request in chat"
      step={1}
      caption="Type your supply amount and target pool"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review the agent's response.** The Blend agent confirms the pool, current APY, and builds the transaction.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-2-supply-response.png"
      alt="Agent response with pool details"
      step={2}
      caption="Agent shows pool APY and prepares the transaction"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review the approval card.** Check the simulation: amount, pool, expected shares received, gas fee.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-3-supply-approval.png"
      alt="Transaction approval card for supply"
      step={3}
      caption="Review simulation results before signing"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Sign in your wallet.** Click Approve, then confirm in Freighter/xBull/Albedo.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-4-supply-sign.png"
      alt="Wallet signature popup"
      step={4}
      caption="Approve the transaction in your wallet extension"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Confirmation.** The agent confirms the transaction succeeded and shows your updated position.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-5-supply-confirmed.png"
      alt="Supply confirmed with updated position"
      step={5}
      caption="Transaction confirmed — your supply is now earning interest"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Borrow Assets

Borrow against your supplied collateral. Your health factor must stay above 1.0.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your borrow request.** Example: "Borrow 200 XLM against my USDC on Blend"

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-1-borrow-prompt.png"
      alt="Typing borrow request"
      step={1}
      caption="Specify the borrow amount and collateral"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review health factor impact.** The agent shows your current health factor and what it will be after borrowing.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-2-borrow-health.png"
      alt="Health factor preview"
      step={2}
      caption="Check that your health factor stays safely above 1.0"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve and sign.** If the health factor is acceptable, approve the transaction.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/blend/step-3-borrow-confirmed.png"
      alt="Borrow confirmed"
      step={3}
      caption="Borrowed assets arrive in your wallet"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
If your health factor drops below 1.0, your collateral can be liquidated. The agent warns you if a borrow would put you in a risky position.
</Callout>

## Check Your Position

Ask the agent about your current Blend status — no transaction required.

**Example prompts:**
- "What's my Blend position?"
- "What's my health factor on Blend?"
- "How much USDC am I earning on Blend?"
- "Show my Blend supply and borrow"

The agent responds with:
- Total supplied (per asset)
- Total borrowed (per asset)
- Current health factor
- Accrued interest
- Claimable BLND rewards
