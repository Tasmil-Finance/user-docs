<TLDR>
The Research, Yield, and Info agents are read-only — they never execute transactions. Use them to compare APYs across protocols, analyze market data, assess risk, get token fundamentals, and navigate the app.
</TLDR>

## Research Agent

Provides market data, protocol comparisons, and token fundamentals.

### What You Can Ask

| Category | Example prompts |
|----------|----------------|
| **Protocol Comparison** | "Compare Blend APR with Soroswap LP fees" |
| **Market Data** | "What's the current XLM price?" |
| **Token Info** | "Tell me about the BLND token" |
| **Pool Analysis** | "Which USDC pools have the highest TVL?" |
| **Risk Assessment** | "What's the risk score for the Blend Fixed pool?" |

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Ask your research question.** Example: "Compare yields across all Stellar protocols for USDC"
