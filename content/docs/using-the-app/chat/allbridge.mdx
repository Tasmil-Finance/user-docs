<TLDR>
The Allbridge agent handles cross-chain bridging. Move USDC, XLM, or other supported assets between Stellar and Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Solana, and more. The agent finds the optimal route with lowest fees and fastest finality.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Bridge Out** | Send assets from Stellar to another chain | "Bridge 100 USDC from Stellar to Ethereum" |
| **Bridge In** | Receive assets from another chain to Stellar | "Bridge 50 USDC from Arbitrum to Stellar" |
| **Check Routes** | Compare bridge routes and fees | "What's the cheapest way to bridge USDC to Ethereum?" |
| **Check Status** | Track a pending bridge transfer | "What's the status of my bridge transfer?" |

## Supported Chains

| Chain | Assets | Estimated Time |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| Ethereum | USDC, USDT | ~15 min |
| Arbitrum | USDC | ~5 min |
| Base | USDC | ~5 min |
| Solana | USDC | ~3 min |
| BNB Chain | USDC, USDT | ~5 min |

## Bridge Assets Out (Stellar → Other Chain)

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your bridge request.** Example: "Bridge 100 USDC from Stellar to Ethereum"

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/allbridge/step-1-bridge-prompt.png"
      alt="Bridge request"
      step={1}
      caption="Specify amount, asset, and destination chain"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review the route.** The agent shows: bridge fee, estimated time, destination address, and minimum received.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/allbridge/step-2-bridge-route.png"
      alt="Bridge route details"
      step={2}
      caption="Review fees, estimated time, and destination details"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Provide destination address.** Enter your wallet address on the destination chain.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/allbridge/step-3-bridge-address.png"
      alt="Enter destination address"
      step={3}
      caption="Paste your wallet address on the destination chain"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve the bridge transaction.** Sign in your Stellar wallet to initiate the transfer.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/allbridge/step-4-bridge-approval.png"
      alt="Bridge approval card"
      step={4}
      caption="Approve the bridge initiation transaction"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Track the transfer.** The agent confirms initiation and provides a tracking link. Assets arrive on the destination chain after the estimated time.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/allbridge/step-5-bridge-tracking.png"
      alt="Bridge transfer tracking"
      step={5}
      caption="Track your transfer until it arrives on the destination chain"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Bridge transfers are irreversible once initiated. Double-check the destination address and chain before approving.
</Callout>
