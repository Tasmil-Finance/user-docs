<TLDR>
The Aquarius agent manages AMM pool operations on Aquarius protocol. Deposit liquidity to earn trading fees plus Aquarius reward incentives, withdraw positions, and claim accumulated rewards.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Add Liquidity** | Deposit tokens into an Aquarius AMM pool | "Add 100 USDC to the Aquarius USDC/XLM pool" |
| **Remove Liquidity** | Withdraw your pool position | "Remove my Aquarius LP position" |
| **Claim Rewards** | Harvest Aquarius incentive rewards | "Claim my Aquarius rewards" |
| **Check Position** | View pool share and accumulated rewards | "What's my Aquarius position?" |

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your deposit request.** Example: "Add 100 USDC and equivalent XLM to Aquarius"
