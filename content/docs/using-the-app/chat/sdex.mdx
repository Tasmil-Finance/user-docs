---

<TLDR>
The SDEX agent operates on Stellar's built-in decentralized exchange orderbook. Place limit orders at specific prices, execute market orders for immediate fills, and manage your open offers.
</TLDR>

## Supported Operations

| Operation | What it does | Example prompt |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Limit Order** | Place an order at a specific price | "Place a limit order: sell 100 USDC at 0.12 XLM each" |
| **Market Order** | Execute immediately at best available price | "Market buy 500 XLM with USDC on SDEX" |
| **Cancel Order** | Remove an open offer | "Cancel my open USDC/XLM offer on SDEX" |
| **Check Orderbook** | View current bids/asks | "Show the USDC/XLM orderbook" |
| **List My Offers** | See your open orders | "What are my open SDEX offers?" |

## Place a Limit Order

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Type your limit order.** Example: "Place a limit order to sell 100 USDC at 8.5 XLM per USDC on SDEX"

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/sdex/step-1-limit-prompt.png"
      alt="Typing limit order"
      step={1}
      caption="Specify the amount, price, and direction (buy/sell)"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review order details.** The agent confirms: asset pair, amount, price, and whether it will fill immediately or sit on the book.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/sdex/step-2-limit-review.png"
      alt="Limit order review"
      step={2}
      caption="Review your order parameters before placing"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve and sign.** The order is placed on the Stellar orderbook.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/chat/sdex/step-3-limit-confirmed.png"
      alt="Limit order placed"
      step={3}
      caption="Order placed — it will fill when the market reaches your price"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
SDEX limit orders have **zero slippage** — they fill at exactly your specified price or not at all. They remain open until filled or cancelled.
</Callout>

## Cancel an Order

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Request cancellation.** Example: "Cancel my open USDC/XLM sell offer on SDEX"
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Confirm which offer.** If you have multiple, the agent lists them for you to pick.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve cancellation.** The offer is removed from the orderbook and funds are released.
  </Step>
</Steps>
