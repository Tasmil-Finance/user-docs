<TLDR>
The Bridge surface moves assets between Stellar and other blockchains (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Solana, BNB Chain) via Allbridge. Select your destination chain, enter the amount, provide a destination address, and approve.
</TLDR>

## Step-by-Step: Bridge Assets

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Navigate to Aggregate → Bridge tab.** Click "Aggregate" in the sidebar, then select the "Bridge" tab.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-1-bridge-tab.png"
      alt="Bridge tab selected"
      step={1}
      caption="Switch to the Bridge tab in the Aggregate surface"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Select source and destination chains.** Choose "Stellar" as source and your destination chain (e.g., Arbitrum).

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-2-select-chains.png"
      alt="Chain selection"
      step={2}
      caption="Pick your source and destination blockchains"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Select the asset.** Choose which token to bridge (e.g., USDC). Only assets supported on both chains are shown.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-3-select-asset.png"
      alt="Asset selection"
      step={3}
      caption="Choose the token to bridge"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Enter the amount.** Type how much you want to bridge. The interface shows the bridge fee and estimated receive amount.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-4-enter-amount.png"
      alt="Enter bridge amount"
      step={4}
      caption="Enter amount — fees and estimated receive shown in real-time"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Enter destination address.** Paste your wallet address on the destination chain. Double-check this — bridge transfers are irreversible.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-5-destination-address.png"
      alt="Enter destination address"
      step={5}
      caption="Paste your wallet address on the destination chain"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review the bridge summary.** Check:
    - Amount to send
    - Bridge fee
    - Amount to receive on destination
    - Estimated arrival time

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-6-review-summary.png"
      alt="Bridge summary"
      step={6}
      caption="Review all details before initiating the bridge"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve in your wallet.** Sign the transaction to lock your assets and initiate the bridge.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-7-approve.png"
      alt="Wallet approval"
      step={7}
      caption="Sign to initiate the bridge transfer"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Track the transfer.** The interface shows transfer status. Assets arrive on the destination chain after the estimated time.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/aggregate/bridge/step-8-tracking.png"
      alt="Transfer tracking"
      step={8}
      caption="Track your bridge transfer until completion"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
**Bridge transfers are irreversible.** Double-check the destination address and chain before approving. Sending to a wrong address means permanent loss of funds.
</Callout>

## Supported Routes

| From | To | Assets | Fee | Time |
|------|-----|--------|-----|------|
| Stellar | Ethereum | USDC, USDT | ~0.3% | ~15 min |
| Stellar | Arbitrum | USDC | ~0.1% | ~5 min |
| Stellar | Base | USDC | ~0.1% | ~5 min |
| Stellar | Solana | USDC | ~0.1% | ~3 min |
| Stellar | BNB Chain | USDC, USDT | ~0.2% | ~5 min |

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Insufficient balance" | Ensure you have enough of the bridged asset plus XLM for gas |
| "Destination address invalid" | Make sure the address format matches the destination chain |
| Transfer stuck | Bridge transfers can take longer during network congestion — wait up to 30 min |
| Didn't receive funds | Check the destination chain's block explorer with your address |
