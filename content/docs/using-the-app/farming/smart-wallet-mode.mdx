<TLDR>
Smart Wallet mode is fully automated. You pick a risk preset (Safe, Balanced, Aggressive), fund your vault, and the AI engine handles everything — pool selection, rebalancing, and reward harvesting. The engine runs every 10 minutes.
</TLDR>

## Step-by-Step: Set Up Smart Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Navigate to Farming → Smart Wallet tab.** Click Farming in the sidebar, then select the "Smart Wallet" mode tab.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-1-navigate.png"
      alt="Smart Wallet tab"
      step={1}
      caption="Select Smart Wallet mode"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Choose your risk preset.** Three options:
    - **Safe** — Blend lending only, no impermanent loss, lower but steady APY
    - **Balanced** — Blend + Soroswap, moderate risk, medium APY
    - **Aggressive** — Blend + Soroswap + Aquarius, higher risk, higher APY

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-2-pick-preset.png"
      alt="Preset picker"
      step={2}
      caption="Compare presets and pick your risk level"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Deploy your keeper wallet** (first time only). Click "Create Vault" — this deploys your personal smart contract on Stellar. Sign the transaction in your wallet.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-3-deploy-vault.png"
      alt="Deploy keeper wallet"
      step={3}
      caption="One-time vault deployment — sign in your wallet"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Wait for deployment confirmation.** Takes 30-60 seconds. The interface shows a loading state, then confirms with your vault address.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-4-deployment-confirmed.png"
      alt="Vault deployed"
      step={4}
      caption="Your keeper wallet is now live on Stellar"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Fund your vault.** Enter the amount of USDC or XLM to deposit. Minimum effective deposit: ~50 USDC.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-5-fund-amount.png"
      alt="Enter fund amount"
      step={5}
      caption="Enter the amount to deposit into your vault"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve the fund transaction.** Sign in your wallet to transfer assets to your keeper wallet.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-6-fund-approve.png"
      alt="Fund approval"
      step={6}
      caption="Approve the deposit transaction"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Engine starts working.** Within 10 minutes, the rebalance engine detects your deposit and deploys it into pools matching your preset. You'll see positions appear in the dashboard.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet/step-7-engine-working.png"
      alt="Engine deploying funds"
      step={7}
      caption="The AI engine deploys your funds into optimal pools"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the Engine Does Automatically

| Action | Frequency | What happens |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Pool Discovery | Every 20 min | Finds new pools, computes risk scores |
| Allocation | Every 10 min | Computes target weights, detects drift, rebalances |
| Position Sync | Every 5 min | Reads on-chain balances, updates dashboard |
| Harvest | Every 4 hours | Claims BLND emissions from lending positions |

## Changing Your Preset

You can switch presets at any time:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Go to Farming → Smart Wallet.** Click the "Change Preset" button.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Select new preset.** The engine will gradually rebalance to the new allocation over the next few cycles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Changing presets doesn't trigger an immediate full rebalance. The engine adjusts gradually over 2-3 cycles to minimize transaction costs.
</Callout>

## Safety Mechanisms

- **30-min cooldown** between rebalances
- **48 rebalances/day max** to prevent excessive trading
- **Halt after 3 consecutive failures** — engine pauses and alerts you
- **Circuit breaker** on >15% TVL drop in a single cycle
