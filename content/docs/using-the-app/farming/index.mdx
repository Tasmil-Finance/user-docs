<TLDR>
Farming is where your assets earn yield. Three modes: **Smart Wallet** (AI auto-rebalances across protocols), **Deposit** (you pick one pool, AI executes), and **Custom** (you set exact allocations). All modes use the keeper-wallet contract for security.
</TLDR>

## Three Modes

| Mode | Best for | AI involvement | Risk |
|------|----------|----------------|------|
| **[Smart Wallet](/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet-mode)** | Set-and-forget users | Full auto — AI picks pools, rebalances, harvests | Varies by preset |
| **[Deposit](/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit-mode)** | Users who know which pool they want | AI executes only — no rebalancing | Pool-specific |
| **[Custom](/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom-mode)** | Advanced users | AI executes your allocation plan | User-defined |

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Click "Farming" in the sidebar.** The Farming page shows your active positions and mode selector.

  <Step>
