title: Custom Mode
description: Set your own allocation percentages across multiple pools
---

<TLDR>
Custom mode gives you full control over allocation. You define exactly what percentage goes to each pool — Blend lending, Soroswap LP, Aquarius, etc. The AI executes your plan and maintains the target weights, but only rebalances when you tell it to.
</TLDR>

## When to Use Custom Mode

- You want specific exposure to multiple protocols
- You have a strategy in mind (e.g., 60% lending, 40% LP)
- You want to control when rebalancing happens

## Step-by-Step: Create a Custom Allocation

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Navigate to Farming → Custom tab.** Select the "Custom" mode from the tabs.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-1-navigate.png"
      alt="Custom mode tab"
      step={1}
      caption="Select Custom mode"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Add pools to your allocation.** Click "Add Pool" and select from available pools. You can add multiple pools from different protocols.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-2-add-pools.png"
      alt="Add pools to allocation"
      step={2}
      caption="Select pools to include in your custom allocation"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Set allocation percentages.** Drag sliders or type percentages for each pool. Total must equal 100%.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-3-set-percentages.png"
      alt="Set allocation percentages"
      step={3}
      caption="Define how much goes to each pool (must total 100%)"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review your allocation plan.** The interface shows:
    - Expected blended APY
    - Risk score (weighted average)
    - Estimated gas costs for deployment

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-4-review-plan.png"
      alt="Allocation plan review"
      step={4}
      caption="Review your blended APY and risk before deploying"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Enter total deposit amount.** The system splits your deposit according to your percentages.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-5-enter-amount.png"
      alt="Enter total deposit"
      step={5}
      caption="Enter the total amount — it splits per your allocation"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve and deploy.** The AI builds multiple transactions (one per pool) and batches them. Sign to execute.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-6-approve-deploy.png"
      alt="Deploy custom allocation"
      step={6}
      caption="Approve the batched deployment transactions"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Allocation active.** Your custom allocation is now live. The dashboard shows each pool's performance individually.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/custom/step-7-active.png"
      alt="Custom allocation active"
      step={7}
      caption="Your custom allocation is deployed and earning"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Custom Allocation

### Rebalance Manually

If pool weights drift from your targets (due to different APYs), you can trigger a manual rebalance:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Click "Rebalance" on the Custom dashboard.** The system shows current vs target weights.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review the rebalance plan.** See which pools will receive more and which will be reduced.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve.** Sign to execute the rebalance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adjust Percentages

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Click "Edit Allocation."** Adjust your percentage sliders.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Save and rebalance.** The engine adjusts positions to match your new targets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Add or Remove Pools

You can add new pools or remove existing ones from your allocation at any time. The system rebalances accordingly.

<Callout type="info">
Custom mode does NOT auto-rebalance. Your allocation drifts naturally as different pools earn at different rates. Trigger rebalance manually when you want to reset to target weights.
</Callout>
