<TLDR>
Deposit mode lets you target a single protocol pool. You pick Blend lending, a Soroswap LP pair, or an Aquarius pool — the AI executes the deposit on-chain. No automatic rebalancing — your funds stay in the pool you chose.
</TLDR>

## When to Use Deposit Mode

- You did your own research and want a specific pool
- You want Blend-only exposure without the engine shifting to other protocols
- You're testing a pool before committing larger amounts

## Step-by-Step: Deposit into a Pool

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Navigate to Farming → Deposit tab.** Select the "Deposit" mode from the tabs.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-1-navigate.png"
      alt="Deposit mode tab"
      step={1}
      caption="Select Deposit mode"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Browse available pools.** The pool list shows all available pools sorted by APY. Filter by:
    - Asset (USDC, XLM)
    - Protocol (Blend, Soroswap, Aquarius, Phoenix)
    - Risk level

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-2-browse-pools.png"
      alt="Pool list with filters"
      step={2}
      caption="Browse and filter pools by asset, protocol, and APY"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Select a pool.** Click on the pool you want. The detail view shows:
    - Current APY (base + incentives)
    - TVL (total value locked)
    - Pool composition
    - Risk score

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-3-pool-details.png"
      alt="Pool detail view"
      step={3}
      caption="Review pool APY, TVL, and risk before depositing"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Enter deposit amount.** Type the amount you want to deposit. Minimum varies by protocol (typically ~10 USDC or XLM).

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-4-enter-amount.png"
      alt="Enter deposit amount"
      step={4}
      caption="Enter your deposit amount"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review and approve.** The AI builds the transaction, simulates it, and shows the confirmation card with expected shares.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-5-approve.png"
      alt="Deposit approval card"
      step={5}
      caption="Review simulation and approve the deposit"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Sign in your wallet.** Confirm the transaction in Freighter/xBull/Albedo.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-6-sign.png"
      alt="Wallet signature"
      step={6}
      caption="Sign the deposit transaction"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Deposit confirmed.** Your position appears in the Farming dashboard. The AI monitors APY and alerts you if it drops significantly.

    <StepScreenshot
      src="/docs/using-the-app/farming/deposit/step-7-confirmed.png"
      alt="Deposit confirmed"
      step={7}
      caption="Your deposit is active and earning yield"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Monitoring Your Position

After depositing, the Farming dashboard shows:
- Current value (principal + earned yield)
- APY (updated in real-time)
- Time in pool
- Claimable rewards (if applicable)

<Callout type="info">
Deposit mode is single-pool — the engine won't rebalance away from your chosen pool. If you want automatic multi-pool management, switch to [Smart Wallet mode](/docs/using-the-app/farming/smart-wallet-mode).
</Callout>

## Withdrawing from Deposit Mode

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Click "Withdraw" on your position.** Enter the amount or click "Max" for full withdrawal.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve the withdrawal.** Sign the transaction. Assets return to your wallet including earned yield.
  </Step>
</Steps>
