<TLDR>
Fifty copy-paste prompts grouped by intent. Open chat, paste one in, and the supervisor routes it to the right agent. Every transaction prompt ends in an approval card — nothing executes until you sign.
</TLDR>

* "Deposit 500 USDC to Aquarius pool"
* "Deploy my vault using Balanced mode"
* "Fund my vault with 100 USDC and allocate to the highest APY pool"

<Callout type="info">
**Reminder:** Every transaction prompt opens an approval card. You inspect the simulation result and choose to sign or reject. The agent cannot move funds without your signature — these prompts set the intent, not the final action.
</Callout>
