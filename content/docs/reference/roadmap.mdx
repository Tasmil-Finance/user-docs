<TLDR>
Three columns: Now (in progress), Next (1-2 months), Later (exploring). Updated when items ship or reprioritize. No dates, no promises — this is a transparency page, not a contract.
</TLDR>

## Now (In Progress)

* **Audit process.** External security audit of keeper-wallet and strategy contracts in progress. Report will be published when complete.
* **Performance fee model.** Designing the High Water Mark-based performance fee structure. Currently no Tasmil fees — this will change in a future release.
* **More screenshots & guides.** Expanding the docs with annotated screenshots for every feature.

## Next (1-2 Months)

* **Quests & Rewards program.** In-app missions with reward tracking.
* **Additional protocol support.** Evaluating new Stellar DeFi protocols for strategy integration.
* **Portfolio CSV export.** One-click export for tax and tracking purposes.
* **Chat prompt library in-app.** Browse prompt recipes directly from the chat interface.

## Later (Exploring)

* **Mobile app.** Native iOS and Android Tasmil apps.
* **Additional chains.** Beyond Stellar — evaluating Arbitrum, Base, and Ethereum.
* **SDK.** Public SDK for developers to integrate Tasmil into their own apps.
* **Governance token.** Community governance model for protocol decisions.

<Callout type="info">
This roadmap reflects current priorities and may change. Watch the [Changelog](/docs/reference/changelog) for confirmed releases.
</Callout>
