## Onboarding

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Do I need a Stellar wallet first?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Yes. Install Freighter, xBull, or Albedo before connecting to Tasmil. See [Set Up Your Wallet](/docs/getting-started/set-up-wallet) in Getting Started.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">How much do I need to start?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Minimum ~5 XLM for account reserve (Stellar requirement) + ~0.5 XLM for vault deployment gas. For the deposit itself, ~50 USDC or XLM is the practical minimum — below that, gas costs eat returns.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Testnet or mainnet first?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Start on testnet. It uses fake tokens and costs nothing. Switch to mainnet once you're familiar with the flow.
</div>
</details>

## Vaults & Yield

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">How long until I see my first APY?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Positions appear within 10 minutes (first rebalance cycle). APY stabilizes after 1-2 position sync cycles (~10-20 minutes total).
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Can I lose money?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Yes, through: impermanent loss on LP positions, protocol smart-contract risk, USDC depeg, or market downturns affecting token prices. Read the [Risks](/docs/trust-and-safety/risks) page for details.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">What happens if the AI makes a bad decision?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
The engine uses APY-proportional weights with risk adjustment — not subjective "intuition." If a pool's APY drops, the engine rebalances away. If 3 consecutive rebalances fail, the vault halts. The AI is formula-driven, not speculative.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Can I change my preset later?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Yes. Switch at any time from Farming settings. The engine rebalances to the new pool set on the next cycle.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Are my funds insured?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
No. Tasmil is non-custodial DeFi — there is no FDIC or insurance of any kind. This is standard for DeFi protocols.
</div>
</details>

## Security

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Does Tasmil have access to my private key?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
No. Your wallet private key never leaves your wallet extension. The AI bot uses session keys you authorize — they have limited scope and you can revoke them at any time.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">What can the bot actually do?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Deposit and withdraw within registered strategy contracts. It cannot send to arbitrary addresses, bypass rate limits, or exceed the daily rebalance cap. Full list on the [What the Bot Can Do](/docs/trust-and-safety/what-the-bot-can-do) page.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">What if Tasmil's servers go down?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Your funds stay in your keeper wallet on-chain. You can withdraw directly via Stellar Explorer using your owner key. The bot stops transacting, but funds are never locked.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">Has the code been audited?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
An external audit is planned. Current status is on the [Audits & Addresses](/docs/trust-and-safety/audits-and-addresses) page.
</div>
</details>

## Fees & Costs

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">What fees does Tasmil charge?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Currently: none beyond Stellar network gas fees. A performance fee (percentage of returns above HWM) is planned but not yet active. See [Fees & Yield](/docs/how-it-works/fees-and-yield).
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">What are the gas costs?</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Typical rebalance: 0.001-0.005 XLM. Vault deployment: ~0.5 XLM. These go to the Stellar network, not Tasmil.
</div>
</details>

## Troubleshooting

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">My positions show empty after depositing.</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Wait 10 minutes for the first rebalance cycle. If still empty after 20 min, check your transaction confirmed on-chain (paste the tx hash in Stellar Expert). If confirmed, check the vault isn't HALTED. See [Troubleshooting](/docs/reference/troubleshooting).
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">A transaction is stuck.</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
If a Soroban transaction is pending beyond 30 seconds, it may have expired due to network congestion. The engine will retry on the next cycle. You don't need to do anything.
</div>
</details>

<details className="border border-fd-border rounded-lg mb-3 group">
<summary className="cursor-pointer px-4 py-3 font-medium text-fd-foreground select-none">The faucet didn't send test XLM.</summary>
<div className="px-4 pb-3 text-sm text-fd-muted-foreground">
Check you used the correct network (testnet mode in wallet + Friendbot). Some faucets have rate limits — wait 5 minutes and try again. If still failing, try the Stellar Quest faucet as an alternative. See [Troubleshooting](/docs/reference/troubleshooting).
</div>
</details>
