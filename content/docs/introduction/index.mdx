<TLDR>
Tasmil Finance is a non-custodial DeFi platform that uses AI-powered agents to manage yield vaults across Stellar/Soroban protocols.
</TLDR>

Tasmil Finance is AI-managed yield on Stellar. Deposit assets, choose a risk preset, and let the autonomous rebalance engine optimize your returns across Blend, Soroswap, Aquarius, and Phoenix.
