<TLDR>
Tasmil supports two base assets (USDC and XLM) deployed across four DeFi protocols on Stellar. More assets and protocols are on the roadmap.
</TLDR>

![Supported protocols on Tasmil](/docs/introduction/protocols-overview.png)

## Base Assets

You can deposit either or both:

| Asset | Network | What you can earn |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **USDC** | Stellar | Lending interest (Blend), LP fees (Soroswap/Aquarius/Phoenix), BLND emissions |
| **XLM** | Stellar | Lending interest (Blend), LP fees (Soroswap/Aquarius/Phoenix), BLND emissions |

Both assets use 7 decimal places on Stellar. When you fund both USDC and XLM, Tasmil deploys each into its own set of pools so you're earning across your whole portfolio.

## Supported Protocols

Tasmil's engine allocates across these four Stellar DeFi protocols:

| Protocol | Type | How you earn | Safe preset | Balanced | Aggressive |
|----------|------|--------------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Blend** | Lending | Supply interest + BLND emissions | Primary | Major | Moderate |
| **Soroswap** | DEX LP | Trading fees | — | Moderate | Major |
| **Aquarius** | AMM | Pool fees | — | Minor | Moderate |
| **Phoenix** | DEX | Pool fees + incentives | — | — | Minor |

Blend lending is the only protocol used by the **Safe** preset — it has zero impermanent loss risk. For higher potential returns, Balanced and Aggressive add DEX liquidity positions from Soroswap, Aquarius, and Phoenix.

Read the full protocol breakdown in [How It Works > Strategies](/docs/how-it-works/strategies).
