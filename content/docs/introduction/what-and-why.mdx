<TLDR>
Tasmil Finance is non-custodial AI-managed DeFi yield vaults on Stellar. Deposit USDC or XLM, pick a risk preset, and the autonomous rebalance engine optimizes returns across Blend, Soroswap, Aquarius, and Phoenix — no manual monitoring required.
</TLDR>

![Tasmil dashboard overview](/docs/introduction/dashboard-overview.png)

## What Tasmil Does

Tasmil takes your deposit and deploys it automatically across Stellar's best DeFi protocols. An AI agent — not you — decides when to move funds, which pool pays the most APY, and when to pull back. You pick a risk level. The engine handles everything else.

**Three things you do:**

* **Deposit.** Send USDC or XLM to your Tasmil vault.
* **Pick a preset.** Choose Safe, Balanced, or Aggressive. That's it.
* **Track.** Watch your portfolio grow. The engine rebalances every 10 minutes.

**Three things the engine does:**

* **Discovers pools.** Every 20 minutes it scans Blend and Soroswap pools for the best risk-adjusted APY.
* **Rebalances.** When one pool drifts more than 5% from its target weight, the engine moves funds.
* **Harvests.** Every 4 hours it claims BLND emissions and re-deploys them into your vault.

## Why Tasmil Is Different

Most yield farming is manual: you check rates, open protocol tabs, swap half your stack, add liquidity, repeat next week. Tasmil automates it on a non-custodial foundation — you keep your keys. The AI bot can only interact with registered strategies. It cannot withdraw to an address that isn't yours.

**Compared to manual yield farming:**

| | Manual Yield Farming | Tasmil Finance |
|---|---|---|
| Time commitment | Weekly rebalancing | None |
| Protocol monitoring | Check rates daily | Engine monitors every 20 min |
| Rebalance decisions | You decide | AI decides, you approve |
| Custody | Your keys (if non-custodial) | Your keys (always non-custodial) |
| Fees | Gas per manual action | Engine-batched, gas-efficient |
| Risk | You pick everything | You pick only the preset |

## Who Tasmil Is For

**Passive earners.** You hold USDC or XLM and want yield without checking pools 3x a day. Deposit, pick Safe, and check in weekly.

**DeFi-curious users.** You're interested in earning from Blend, Soroswap, Aquarius, and Phoenix but don't know which pool to pick. Tasmil's AI picks for you based on real on-chain data.

**Stellar holders.** You already hold XLM and want it to work. Tasmil deploys XLM alongside USDC into yield-earning positions. You can fund both and track them side-by-side.

**Not for you if:** you want full manual control over every pool allocation (try the Custom mode in Farming) or you're looking for a bank-style guaranteed-return product.
