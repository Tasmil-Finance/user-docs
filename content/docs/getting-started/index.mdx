Get up and running with Tasmil Finance in minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A Stellar wallet (Freighter, Lobstr, or xBull)
- XLM for transaction fees (minimum 5 XLM recommended)
- Assets to deposit (USDC, XLM, or other supported tokens)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Connect your wallet** — Link your Freighter, Lobstr, or xBull wallet to the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Choose a risk preset** — Select Safe, Balanced, or Aggressive based on your risk tolerance.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Make your first deposit** — Deposit assets into your vault and the AI engine handles the rest.
  </Step>
</Steps>
